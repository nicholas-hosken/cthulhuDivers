Xplor Adventure Park

Transportation Included

From: $160

Xplor is an All-Inclusive Park where you have the opportunity to enjoy various breathtaking adventures in the exquisite biodiversity that only the Mayan jungle can offer. Fly across the sky on Zip Lines, test your driving skills in our amphibious ATV’s on heart-racing bush trails, swim and raft through impressive rock formations in natural caverns and caves before finally splashing into cenotes on a swinging hammock!

It's time to get your heart racing!

Schedule

You’ll be picked up between 7am and 8am depending on the location of your hotel or AirBnB. We’ll aim to have you soon after the park opens at 9am so you have the entire day to Xplor the park unit it closes at 5pm.

Includes

  • Extensive Buffet Lunch starting at 11:00 a.m. – 5:00 p.m.
  • Two circuits with 14 zip-lines.
  • Amphibious ATV’s which you can use to Xplor two different three mile circuits through the jungle.
  • Two underground river circuits to paddle on rafts.
  • Cavern Walk with unparalleled underground scenery.
  • All equipment needed for your safety (helmet, harness, life jacket, raft, and hand paddles).
  • Use of Locker Rooms, Locker with Key, Showers.

Important Info

  • Minimum age of 5 years to enter Xplor Park.
  • Children under 12 years of age with a height greater than 4’5” must present identification to prove their age.
  • Zip Line activities require minimum height of 3’6”, minimum weight of 88lbs, and maximum weight of 300lbs. Guests who do not meet these requirements will not be able to carry out the activity for their safety.

Transportation

When you purchase tickets to any Xcaret Park ( Xcaret, Xel-Ha, Xplor, Xplor Fuego, Xenotes, Xoximilco, Xenses, or Xavage ) roundtrip transportation will be provided from your Hotel.

Recommendations

  • Bring proof of ticket purchase.
  • Wear comfortable clothes, your swimsuit, and pack extra clothes to change into.
  • If you are pregnant, have heart problems, fear of heights, recent surgery, or weigh more than 300lbs. Certain activities in the park are not recommended.
  • Bring a Towel and Biodegradable Sunscreen** Help us to care for and preserve our ecosystem! In order not to affect the natural balance of the ecosystem, the Park is only allowed to use sunscreens and tanning agents that contain titanium oxide and zinc oxide. We invite you to use of biodegradable and chemical-free products!
  • Bring Cash or Cards to purchase Souvenirs and Photo Album of your visit.
