WELCOME TO
CTHULHU DIVERS

Created with the aim of providing a more personalized diving experience for small groups, our store offers some of the most intimate excursions to the incredible underwater destinations found locally in Cancun and the greater Riviera Maya. Frequented dive sites include: the cenotes, Cozumel and, of course, Cancun's Underwater Museum. Our team of instructors, divemasters and boat crew are each armed with the experience and local knowledge to insure that you'll get the most out of your dives. We also provide well-maintained and up-to-date equipment with all diving courses being presented by fully certified PADI instructors.
