SCUBA DIVING IN CANCUN, PLAYA MUJERES, PLAYA DEL CARMEN AND TULUM

The stunningly beautiful tropical cities of Cancun, Playa Mujeres, Playa del Carmen and Tulum are the pearls of the Atlantic Ocean and can be found on the easternmost edge of Mexico and are surrounded by world-class diving locations such as Isla Mujeres, Cozumel and, of course, the mystical cenotes. The edge of our white sandy beaches are caressed by the gorgeous turquoise waters of the Caribbean. Its in these waters you’ll find some of the healthiest reefs which are absolutely resplendent with life. It is the perfect destination for travelers who love the ocean and who wish to experience a true Latin underwater paradise.

Whether you wish to learn to dive in Cancun at Musa, the world’s largest underwater museum, or are a certified diver on the hunt for a high quality, great value daily dive excursions, you need look no further than Cthulhu Divers. Our international and highly experienced multilingual team of instructors, divemasters and boat crew will ensure that you get the most out of your diving holiday, whether that means earning a PADI diving license, enjoying the thrill of a weeks liveaboard luxury adventure or the flexibility of daily diving from one of our dive boats. We also provide well-maintained and up-to-date equipment with all diving courses being presented by fully certified PADI instructors.

For the super adventurous, we also offer cavern diving, cave diving and cenote diving. On these private tours, you will travel out deep into the Quintana Roo Jungle where you will be accompanied by specialized cave certified diver as you enter the ancient Mayan underworld. Here you will get to explore a realm which most can’t even imagine.

For a taste of local history, we also offer tours from Cancun & Playa Mujeres to Chichén Itzá, Tulum and Coba. Alternatively for a more family orientated day in the sun, our tours to Xcaret, Xplor, Xel-Ha, Xavage, Xoximilco and Xenses are all fantastic options found in our ‘Landlubber Adventure’ category.

Cthulhu Divers operates from dive centers in Playa Mujeres and Cancun. Each with the same philosophy: to ensure that you get the absolute best scuba diving and snorkeling experience obtainable!
