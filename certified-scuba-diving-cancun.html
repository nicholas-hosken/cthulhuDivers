If variety is the spice of life then Cancun is the spiciest diving city in all of Mexico. Wreck dives, drift dives, night dives, cavern dives, cave dives, shallow dives, deep dives and bull shark dives are all available within two hour travel from our store. Explore our various dive sites below and begin your adventure tomorrow.

Cancun & Playa Mujeres

Our local dives sites are all protected within "Cancun & Isla Mujeres National Park" and are therefore in a very healthy condition. This has resulted in us commonly seeing of a wide array of sea life which might be very rare to non-existent in other parts of the Caribbean.

We begin each morning with a two tank dive trip at two of our deep dive (19m+/63ft+) sites. This is promptly followed by another two tank dive trip in the afternoon, either on two shallow reefs or Cancun's Under Water Museum and a nearby shallow reef.

Single tank night dives sites and times vary throughout the year according to season and demand. However we endeavour to enter the water just before sunset as this is one of the most active times for viewing fish mating rituals. Get in touch with us for more info.

Puerto Morelos

The oldest city in the Mexican state of Quintana Roo, Puerto Morelos has a number of spectacular shallow reefs located within the "Puerto Morelos National Reef Park" which make for some excellent diving both for beginners and for divers who have been out of the water for a while.

However the crowning glory of this little fishing village is the wreck dive on the C-56 Juan Escutia. This minesweeper which was sunk in 2000 was specifically prepared for divers to explore safely. This means that, with the supervision of a guide, you can safely penetrate the wreck without any additional training!
